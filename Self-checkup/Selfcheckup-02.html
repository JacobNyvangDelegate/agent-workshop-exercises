Adding Knowledge: Recipes, Rules, and Reviews

Use this self-check to confirm that you understand how knowledge sources help the agent answer questions from uploaded files and unstructured content.

Questions

  1. Why do we upload recipe, allergen, and staff handbook PDFs as knowledge sources?
  2. What does it mean that the agent must wait for the files to be indexed?
  3. What is the difference between asking about a recipe and asking about customer reviews?
  4. Why is it useful to test the agent with questions across multiple knowledge sources?

🎯 Checkpoint

  • ✅ You understand how to add file-based knowledge
  • ✅ You know why indexing is required
  • ✅ You can test answers from structured documents and messy content
  • ✅ You understand how knowledge expands what the agent can answer
