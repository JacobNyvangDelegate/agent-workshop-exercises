Lab 2 · ~20 minutes

Adding Knowledge: Recipes, Rules, and Reviews

SweetBite Bakery wants the agent to handle recipes, allergens, HR rules, and customer feedback. You’ll upload bakery PDFs and a customer reviews page to expand its knowledge.

🥐 What you’ll do

  • Upload PDFs to the knowledge base
  • Test generative answers across multiple sources
  • Add customer reviews as an unstructured source

Step 1: Upload bakery documents

  1. Go to Overview.
  2. Scroll to Knowledge.
  3. Click + Add knowledge.
  4. Choose File upload.
  5. Upload from /data:
    • SweetBite_RecipeBook.pdf
    • SweetBite_AllergenPolicy.pdf
    • SweetBite_StaffHandbook.pdf
  6. Add short descriptions.
  7. Wait for indexing.

Expected result

Step 2: Test with multiple knowledge sources

Ask your agent:

  • “Give me the recipe for chocolate cake.”
  • “What allergens are in croissants?”
  • “How do I request vacation days as a baker?”

Expected result

Step 3: Add customer reviews

Now let’s add some messy content to see how generative AI handles it.

  1. Go back to Overview > Knowledge > + Add knowledge.
  2. Choose File upload.
  3. Upload:
    SweetBite_CustomerReviews.html
  4. Description:
    Customer reviews and ratings from bakery website.
  5. Save and wait for indexing.

Step 4: Test with reviews included

Ask your agent questions that rely on opinions instead of rules:

  • “What do customers think about the croissants?”
  • “Have people mentioned anything about gluten-free options?”
  • “How do customers feel about the staff?”

🎯 Checkpoint

  • ✅ Added 3 bakery PDFs
  • ✅ Added unstructured customer reviews file
  • ✅ Tested recipe, allergen, HR queries, and customer opinions
